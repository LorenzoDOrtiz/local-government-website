Discovering Olympia

alt="Washington state map">

Olympia, nestled in the Pacific Northwest, is a city of captivating contrasts. Explore the Capitol Building, stroll along the waterfront, and experience the unique blend of tradition and modernity that defines Olympia. The city boasts a vibrant cultural tapestry woven from diverse communities, each contributing to the overall vibrancy that defines our capital.

Facts about Olympia

  • City's Population:

    As of the last estimate in 2022, Olympia's population is approximately 52,000.

  • City Incorporation Date:

    Olympia became a city on November 28, 1859.

  • Region:

    Olympia is a part of the Puget Sound region, contributing to the overall diversity of Washington State.

  • Cityscape:

    Olympia boasts a mix of urban and suburban areas, providing a high quality of life for its residents. The city's architecture and green spaces reflect its commitment to sustainability and environmental stewardship.

  • Cultural Scene:

    Explore Olympia's thriving cultural scene, from museums to theaters, showcasing the city's commitment to creativity and discovery. Visit institutions like the Washington State Capitol and the Hands On Children's Museum to experience the rich cultural tapestry of Olympia.

Explore Olympia

  1. Olympia Overview

    Olympia: the charming capital city of Washington. Immerse yourself in its rich history, cultural heritage, and picturesque landscapes. Explore the Capitol Building, stroll along the waterfront, and experience the unique blend of tradition and modernity that defines Olympia.

  2. Top Attractions

    Discover Olympia's top attractions, including the iconic Capitol Building, Percival Landing Park, and the Hands On Children's Museum. Visit the Washington State Capitol to witness the seat of government and explore the vibrant local culture at popular landmarks.

  3. Local Cuisine

    Delight your taste buds with Olympia's diverse culinary scene. From fresh seafood at Anthony's HomePort to local favorites at Waterstreet Cafe, the city offers a variety of dining experiences. Don't miss the chance to explore the Olympia Farmers Market for fresh, local produce.
